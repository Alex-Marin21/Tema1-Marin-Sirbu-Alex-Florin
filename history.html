The History of Audi

Key Milestones

  • 1909: Founded by August Horch after leaving his previous company.
    • 1910: The first Audi automobile, the Audi Type A, was produced.
  • 1932: Audi merged with three other companies to form Auto Union, introducing the four rings logo.
  • 1965: Audi became part of the Volkswagen Group, leading to increased resources and technology sharing.
  • 1980: The launch of the Audi Quattro revolutionized all-wheel drive technology.
    • 1982: The Audi Quattro won the World Rally Championship.
  • 2006: Audi introduced the first luxury SUV, the Audi Q7, expanding into the SUV market.

Recent Developments

In recent years, Audi has focused on sustainability and electric vehicles, introducing models such as:

  • 2018: The launch of the Audi e-tron, Audi's first fully electric SUV.
  • 2020: Introduction of the Audi Q4 e-tron, expanding their electric vehicle lineup.
  • 2021: Audi's commitment to becoming carbon neutral by 2050.
