Ferdinand Porsche 75

August Horch 81

Founder of Audi and one of the pioneers in automotive engineering.

  • Founded Audi in 1910.

Former CEO of Daimler AG, influenced Audi's development through industry competition.

  • Led Mercedes-Benz during key periods of rivalry with Audi.
  • Known for his innovation-driven leadership.
Rupert Stadler 60

Former CEO of Audi AG, helped drive innovation in electric vehicles.

Walter Röhrl 77

Legendary rally driver, instrumental in promoting Audi's Quattro technology.

  • Two-time World Rally Champion.
  • Promoted the Audi Quattro in motorsport.
