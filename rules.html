Rules and Regulations of Audi

General Conduct

  • All drivers must hold a valid driver's license.
  • Speed limits must be adhered to at all times.
  • Safety gear is required when driving Audi sports cars.

Vehicle Maintenance

Click here for vehicle maintenance rules

Proper vehicle maintenance is crucial for safety and performance. Key maintenance rules include:

  • Regular oil changes every 5,000 miles.
  • Check tire pressure monthly.
  • Brake systems should be inspected bi-annually.

Driving Events

Participation in Audi driving events is governed by specific rules:

  • Drivers must register in advance.
  • Participation is subject to vehicle inspection.
  • Insurance coverage is required for all participants.
